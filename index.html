Bio

   My name is M and I'm a senior pursuing my Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science at DePaul University, with a double minor in both audio recording and data science. Currently working as a Supplemental Instructor within my designated college, I spend much of my free time working on lesson planning and engaging with students in Systems I. It's become one of my greatest priorities to help other students within my major in hopes that I can motivate them as my peers have helped me with challenging material in the past.

Beyond academics, my interests include composing sound art with a focus in recording techniques, as well as bike riding when the weather in Chicago permits.

Extracurriculars

I serve as an organizer and graphics designer for DePaul's annual hackathon called DemonHacks. I'm also a member of DePaul's Computer Science Society.
